How to caluculate Ranking.

Do you know how to calculate these ranking? When I saw this ranking, I was wondering how to determine this rank. It was described in Forbes webpage. This one was usually calculated the number of Stock and the price of Stock. Furthermore, Market capitalizations also important because This size will determine how much company can grow up more or not. For example, IT's market is increasing because this is new technology. However, old industry's demand and supply will shrink and it would someday have disappeared. It is calculated from the share price (as recorded on selected day) multiplied by the number of outstanding shares. Figures are converted into USD millions (using rate from selected day) to allow for comparison. Only companies with free float at least 15% are included, value of unlisted stock classes is excluded. Investment companies are not included in the list
